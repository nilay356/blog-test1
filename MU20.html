MU20 Opportunity Summit 2026 — Asia’s Biggest High School Conference

Updated · 2025

The MU20 Opportunity Summit is one of the most influential student conferences in Asia, bringing together thousands of high school leaders, innovators, creators, and changemakers under one roof. Known for its large-scale impact and global participation, the summit continues to grow in size and prestige every year.

About the MU20 Opportunity Summit

The MU20 Summit focuses on empowering high school students through competitive challenges, leadership-building activities, and hands-on workshops. It is designed to help students develop essential skills such as communication, problem-solving, creativity, and decision-making.

Focus Areas

  • Leadership development
  • Innovation and entrepreneurship
  • Real-world problem solving
  • Career exploration and personal growth

Activities

Participants engage in a diverse set of competitions and workshops, including:

  • Model United Nations (MUN)
  • Entrepreneurship and business challenges
  • Hackathons & innovation sprints
  • Debate & public speaking
  • Theatre arts and creative performance

The MU20 School of Opportunity

MU20 is the organization behind the summit, dedicated to creating meaningful opportunities for young leaders. They run multiple programs throughout the year, all aimed at helping students unlock their potential and gain global exposure.

“Asia’s biggest high school conference, connecting over 100 schools and thousands of students worldwide.”

MU20 Opportunity Summit 2026

While full details for the 2026 summit are not yet officially published, the organizers have confirmed that the event will return:

“See you next year at the MU20 Opportunity Summit 2026 — bigger, bolder, and brighter.”

The 2026 edition is expected to continue its legacy of:

  • Huge student participation
  • Global school representation
  • Top-tier workshops & challenges
  • Leadership & career-focused sessions

MEGA Hackathon 2026
